OUR STORY

From passion to profession - that's the journey of Cekdika Autocare.

Founded in 2021 by Dika, an automotive enthusiast with a keen eye for detail, Cekdika Autocare started as a small side business with just basic equipment and a big dream. What began as washing friends' cars in a driveway has grown into a professional automotive care service trusted by hundreds of customers.

Our founder's journey on social media platforms like TikTok and Instagram helped share our passion with the world, showcasing the art and science behind proper car detailing. Today, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to deliver exceptional results.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional, personalized auto care services that enhance vehicle longevity, safety, and customer satisfaction. We strive to exceed expectations with every detail, using premium products and proven techniques to protect and enhance your vehicle.

Our Vision

To become Malaysia's most trusted and preferred automotive care provider, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer-centric approach. We aim to set the standard for automotive detailing in Southeast Asia.

DIKA

MEET OUR FOUNDER

Dika's passion for cars began at a young age, influenced by his father who was a mechanic. After years of studying automotive engineering and working in various car workshops, he identified a gap in the market for premium, detail-oriented car care services.

What sets Dika apart is his hands-on approach - he's personally involved in every major detailing job and continuously experiments with new techniques and products to achieve better results. His attention to detail and commitment to quality have earned Cekdika Autocare its reputation for excellence.
